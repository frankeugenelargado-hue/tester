Unova Region

As usual, in Black2/White2 you battle your way through 8 gyms, each specializing in a particular type. Following that you take on the Elite Four.

Gym Leaders

  • Cheren - Aspertia City - Normal Type
  • Roxie - Virbank City - Poison Type
  • Burgh - Castelia City - Electric Type
  • Elesa - Nimbasa City - Electric Type
  • Clay - Driftveil City - Ground Type
  • Skyla - Mistralton City - Flying Type
  • Drayden - Opelucid City - Dragon Type
  • Marlon - Humilau City - Water Type

Elite Four

  • Shauntal - Ghost Type
  • Grimsley - Dark Type
  • Caitlin - Psychic Type
  • Marshal - Fighting Type

Gym Leaders

First Gym Leader

Cheren - Aspertia City - Normal Type

Cheren is the first Gym Leader you will face in the Unova region. He specializes in Normal-type Pokémon and is known for his strong defense.

Pokémon Team:

  • Patrat - Level 12
  • Lillipup - Level 14

Roxie - Virbank City - Poison Type

Roxie is the second Gym Leader in the Unova region. She specializes in Poison-type Pokémon and uses speed and agility to her advantage.

Pokémon Team:

  • Koffing - Level 17
  • Whirlipede - Level 20

Burgh - Castelia City - Bug Type

Burgh is the third Gym Leader in the Unova region. He specializes in Bug-type Pokémon and is known for his artistic flair.

Pokémon Team:

  • Dwebble - Level 24
  • Leavanny - Level 22
  • Swadloon - Level 26

Elesa - Nimbasa City - Electric Type

Elesa is the fourth Gym Leader in the Unova region. She specializes in Electric-type Pokémon and uses powerful electric attacks to overwhelm her opponents.

Pokémon Team:

  • Emolga - Level 28
  • Flaaffy - Level 30
  • Zebstrika - Level 32

Clay - Driftveil City - Ground Type

Clay is the fifth Gym Leader in the Unova region. He specializes in Ground-type Pokémon and is known for his powerful physical attacks.

Pokémon Team:

  • Excadrill - Level 31
  • Krokorok - Level 34
  • Sandslash - Level 32

Skyla - Mistralton City - Flying Type

Skyla is the sixth Gym Leader in the Unova region. She specializes in Flying-type Pokémon and uses powerful aerial attacks to overwhelm her opponents.

Pokémon Team:

  • Swanna - Level 33
  • Skarmory - Level 38
  • Swoobat - Level 36

Drayden - Mistralton City - Dragon Type

Drayden is the seventh Gym Leader in the Unova region. He specializes in Dragon-type Pokémon and uses powerful dragon attacks to overwhelm his opponents.

Pokémon Team:

  • Druddigon - Level 42
  • Flygon - Level 35
  • Haxorus - Level 38

Marlon - Humilau City - Water Type

Marlon is the eighth and final Gym Leader in the Unova region. He specializes in Water-type Pokémon and uses a combination of powerful attacks and evasive maneuvers.

Pokémon Team:

  • Wailord - Level 45
  • Jellicent - Level 42
  • Carracosta - Level 47

Elite Four

Shauntal - Dark Type

Shauntal is the first member of the Elite Four in the Unova region. She specializes in Dark-type Pokémon and uses a combination of status effects and powerful dark attacks.

Pokémon Team:

  • Cofagrigus - Level 52
  • Drifblim - Level 51
  • Golurk - Level 54
  • Chandelure - Level 53

Rematch/Second Battle

  • Cofagrigus - Level 57
  • Drifblim - Level 56
  • Golurk - Level 59
  • Chandelure - Level 58
  • Mismagius - Level 60
  • Froslass - Level 56

Grimsley - Dark Type

Grimsley is the second member of the Elite Four in the Unova region. He specializes in Dark-type Pokémon and uses a variety of status moves to wear down his opponents.

Pokémon Team:

  • Liepard - Level 51
  • Bisharp - Level 53
  • Krookodile - Level 55
  • Scrafty - Level 57

Rematch/Second Battle

  • Liepard - Level 57
  • Krookodile - Level 59
  • Houndoom - Level 59
  • Honchkrow - Level 61
  • Bisharp - Level 63
  • Scrafty - Level 65

Caitlin - Psychic Type

Caitlin is the third member of the Elite Four in the Unova region. She specializes in Psychic-type Pokémon and uses a variety of mind-affecting moves to control the battlefield.

Pokémon Team:

  • Sigilyph - Level 54
  • Musharna - Level 53
  • Gothitelle - Level 52
  • Reuniclus - Level 56

Rematch/Second Battle

  • Gallade - Level 59
  • Gothitelle - Level 58
  • Metagross - Level 57
  • Reuniclus - Level 60
  • Sigilyph - Level 62
  • Musharna - Level 64

Marshal - Fighting Type

Marshal is the final member of the Elite Four in the Unova region. He specializes in Fighting-type Pokémon and uses powerful physical attacks to overwhelm his opponents.

Pokémon Team:

  • Throh - Level 54
  • Sawk - Level 56
  • Mienshao - Level 58
  • Conkeldurr - Level 60

Rematch/Second Battle

  • Throh - Level 59
  • Sawk - Level 61
  • Mienshao - Level 63
  • Conkeldurr - Level 65
  • Lucario - Level 67
  • Medicham - Level 69
