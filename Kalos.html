Gym Leaders

First Gym Leader

Viola - Santalune City - Bug Type

Viola is the first Gym Leader you will face in the Kalos region. She specializes in Bug-type Pokémon and is known for her strong defense.

Pokémon Team:

  • Surskit - Level 12
  • Vivillon - Level 14

Grant - Cyllage City - Rock Type

Grant is the second Gym Leader in the Kalos region. He specializes in Rock-type Pokémon and uses speed and agility to his advantage.

Pokémon Team:

  • Amaura - Level 17
  • Tyrunt - Level 20

Korrina - Cyllage City - Fighting Type

Korrina is the third Gym Leader in the Kalos region. She specializes in Fighting-type Pokémon and is known for her powerful attacks.

Pokémon Team:

  • Hawlucha - Level 24
  • Machoke - Level 22
  • Mienfoo - Level 26

Ramos - Coumarine City - Grass Type

Ramos is the fourth Gym Leader in the Kalos region. He specializes in Grass-type Pokémon and uses powerful grass attacks to overwhelm his opponents.

Pokémon Team:

  • Gogoat - Level 28
  • Jumpluff - Level 30
  • Weepinbell - Level 32

Clemont - Lumiose City - Electric Type

Clemont is the fifth Gym Leader in the Kalos region. He specializes in Electric-type Pokémon and is known for his powerful electric attacks.

Pokémon Team:

  • Heliolisk - Level 31
  • Emolga - Level 34
  • Magneton - Level 32

Valerie - Laverre City - Fairy Type

Valerie is the sixth Gym Leader in the Kalos region. She specializes in Fairy-type Pokémon and uses powerful fairy attacks to overwhelm her opponents.

Pokémon Team:

  • Sylveon - Level 33
  • Mawile - Level 36
  • Mr. Mime - Level 34

Olympia - Anistar City - Psychic Type

Olympia is the seventh Gym Leader in the Kalos region. She specializes in Psychic-type Pokémon and uses a combination of powerful psychic attacks and evasive maneuvers.

Pokémon Team:

  • Meowstic (Male) - Level 42
  • Sigilyph - Level 35
  • Slowking - Level 38

Wulfric - Snowbelle - Ice Type

Wulfric is the eighth and final Gym Leader in the Kalos region. He specializes in Ice-type Pokémon and uses a combination of powerful attacks and evasive maneuvers.

Pokémon Team:

  • Abomasnow - Level 45
  • Avalugg - Level 42
  • Cryogonal - Level 47

Elite Four

Malva - Fire Type

Malva is the first member of the Elite Four in the Kalos region. She specializes in Fire-type Pokémon and uses a combination of status effects and powerful fire attacks.

Pokémon Team:

  • Talonflame - Level 52
  • Chandelure - Level 51
  • Pyroar - Level 54
  • Torkoal - Level 53

Siebold - Water Type

Siebold is the second member of the Elite Four in the Kalos region. He specializes in Water-type Pokémon and uses a variety of status moves to wear down his opponents.

Pokémon Team:

  • Barbaracle - Level 51
  • Gyarados - Level 53
  • Clawitzer - Level 53
  • Starmie - Level 55

Wikstrom - Steel Type

Wikstrom is the third member of the Elite Four in the Kalos region. He specializes in Steel-type Pokémon and uses a variety of defensive strategies to outlast his opponents.

Pokémon Team:

  • Klefki - Level 54
  • Probopass - Level 53
  • Scizor - Level 52
  • Aegislash - Level 56

Drasna - Dragon Type

Drasna is the final member of the Elite Four in the Kalos region. She specializes in Dragon-type Pokémon and uses powerful attacks to overwhelm her opponents.

Pokémon Team:

  • Altaria - Level 54
  • Dragalge - Level 56
  • Druddigon - Level 58
  • Noivern - Level 60
