Featured Regions

Kanto Regions

The Kanto region features a unique scenery that comes along with diverse numbers of Pokemon.

Johto Region

The Johto region is known for its rich history and the introduction of new Pokemon species.

Hoenn Region

The Hoenn region is famous for its diverse landscapes, including lush forests and vast oceans.

Sinnoh Region

The Sinnoh region is characterized by its mountainous terrain and ancient legends.

Unova Region

The Unova region is inspired by modern metropolitan areas and features a wide variety of urban environments.

Kalos Region

The Kalos region is known for its fashion and culture, as well as its connection to Mega Evolution.

About Us

City Badge Chronicles is dedicated to providing comprehensive information about the various Pokémon regions, their gyms, and the challenges trainers face. Our mission is to help trainers of all levels navigate the exciting world of Pokémon and achieve their goals.
