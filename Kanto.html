Kanto Region

As usual, in FireRed/LeafGreen you battle your way through 8 gyms, each specializing in a particular type. Following that you take on the Elite Four and the Champion. The teams are mostly the same as the original Red/Blue with a few level changes. The levels of the Elite Four's Pokémon also increase from the second challenge onward.

Gym Leaders

  • Brock - Pewter City - Rock Type
  • Misty - Cerulean City - Water Type
  • Lt. Surge - Vermilion City - Electric Type
  • Erika - Celadon City - Grass Type
  • Koga - Fuchsia City - Poison Type
  • Sabrina - Saffron City - Psychic Type
  • Blaine - Cinnabar Island - Fire Type
  • Giovanni - Viridian City - Ground Type

Elite Four

  • Lorelei - Ice Type
  • Bruno - Fighting Type
  • Agatha - Ghost Type
  • Lance - Dragon Type

Gym Leaders

First Gym Leader

Brock - Pewter City - Rock Type

Brock is the first Gym Leader you will face in the Kanto region. He specializes in Rock-type Pokémon and is known for his defensive strategies.

Pokémon Team:

  • Geodude - Level 12
  • Onix - Level 14

Misty - Cerulean City - Water Type

Misty is the second Gym Leader in the Kanto region. She specializes in Water-type Pokémon and uses speed and agility to her advantage.

Pokémon Team:

  • Staryu - Level 18
  • Starmie - Level 21

Lt. Surge - Vermilion City - Electric Type

Lt. Surge is the third Gym Leader in the Kanto region. He specializes in Electric-type Pokémon and is known for his powerful attacks.

Pokémon Team:

  • Pikachu - Level 21
  • Raichu - Level 24

Erika - Celadon City - Grass Type

Erika is the fourth Gym Leader in the Kanto region. She specializes in Grass-type Pokémon and uses status effects to weaken her opponents.

Pokémon Team:

  • Tangela - Level 24
  • Victreebel - Level 32
  • Vileplume - Level 32

Koga - Fuchsia City - Poison Type

Koga is the fifth Gym Leader in the Kanto region. He specializes in Poison-type Pokémon and uses a variety of status moves to confuse and poison his opponents.

Pokémon Team:

  • Koffing - Level 37
  • Weezing - Level 39
  • Muk - Level 39

Sabrina - Saffron City - Psychic Type

Sabrina is the sixth Gym Leader in the Kanto region. She specializes in Psychic-type Pokémon and uses powerful psychic attacks to overwhelm her opponents.

Pokémon Team:

  • Kadabra - Level 38
  • Alakazam - Level 43
  • Mr. Mime - Level 41

Blaine - Cinnabar Island - Fire Type

Blaine is the seventh Gym Leader in the Kanto region. He specializes in Fire-type Pokémon and uses high-speed attacks to catch his opponents off guard.

Pokémon Team:

  • Ponyta - Level 42
  • Rapidash - Level 45
  • Arcanine - Level 47

Giovanni - Viridian City - Ground Type

Giovanni is the eighth and final Gym Leader in the Kanto region. He specializes in Ground-type Pokémon and uses a combination of powerful attacks and defensive strategies.

Pokémon Team:

  • Rhyhorn - Level 45
  • Dugtrio - Level 42
  • Nidoking - Level 47
